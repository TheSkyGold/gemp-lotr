Fellowship block
  • cards from sets 01-03 are legal,
  • sites from Fellowship block are used,
  • Ring-bearer skirmish CAN be cancelled,
  • there is no X-list,
  • R-list has one card - Forces of Mordor.
The Two Towers block
  • cards from sets 04-06 are legal,
  • sites from The Two Towers block are used,
  • Ring-bearer skirmish CAN be cancelled,
  • there is no X-list,
  • there is no R-list.
